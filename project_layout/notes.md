* Large Application with Internal Packages

In larger applications, you may have one or more internal packages that are either tied together with a main runner script or that provide specific functionality to a larger library you are packaging.

```
helloworld/
│
├── bin/
│
├── docs/
│   ├── hello.md
│   └── world.md
│
├── helloworld/
│   ├── __init__.py
│   ├── runner.py
│   ├── hello/
│   │   ├── __init__.py
│   │   ├── hello.py
│   │   └── helpers.py
│   │
│   └── world/
│       ├── __init__.py
│       ├── helpers.py
│       └── world.py
│
├── data/
│   ├── input.csv
│   └── output.xlsx
│
├── tests/
│   ├── hello
│   │   ├── helpers_tests.py
│   │   └── hello_tests.py
│   │
│   └── world/
│       ├── helpers_tests.py
│       └── world_tests.py
│
├── .gitignore
├── LICENSE
└── README.md
```
